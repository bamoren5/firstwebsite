Green, like blue, is also a cool color according to tigercolorâ€™s website. Also, like blue, and well every other color, the significance of green is vastly different across the world. In Western cultures, green represents spring, money, greed, and much more. Most Asian and eastern cultures relate green with new and eternal life, fertility, youth, health. On a more sad note, some South American cultures that have a lot of forests symbolize the color with death. Ultimately the color can also symbolize balance and harmony. Some positive feelings related with the color included, renewal and restoration, self-reliance, reliability and dependability.
