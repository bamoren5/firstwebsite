Blue

Go to W3C Validation Icon

The color blue is a cool color according tigercolor's website. It can also represents a lot of different emotions throughout the world. For example, westerners associate the color with feeling "melancholy" which is where the term "having the blues" comes from. However, in Middle Eastern countries the color signifies safety and protection and symbolizes heaven, spirituality and immortality. When it comes to our mood, it is said that blue can reduce stress and creates a sense of calmness for us. Bourncreative states that the color also reprints the sky and the sea, and is linked to freedom, imagination, and sensitivity.
