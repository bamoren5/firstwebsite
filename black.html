Black

Go to W3C Validation Icon

When it comes to black it isn’t a warm or cool color, it’s a neutral color. In a lot of cultures, black symbolizes death, but it can also symbolize sophistication and formality (i.e. tuxedos). In middle eastern cultures, black can symbolize rebirth and mourning and in Africa it can stand for age, maturity, and masculinity. On a psychology level, black means control, power, and it relates to being hidden or secretive. Some fun fats include that black is just the absence of color, so some might say that it isn’t a color at all. It is associated with the unknown.
